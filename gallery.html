RECIPES

Roasted Squash Houmous

Roasted squash, combined with chickpeas and tahini and served with pitta, makes a lovely dip to accompany drinks or, alongside a salad, a nice light lunch.

Bush’s Grilled stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Top your grilled sweet potatoes with the heartiness of BUSH'S® Country Style Baked Beans, the smokiness of bacon and the sweetness of peaches for a unique side

Corn Tortilla Chicken Lasagna

This Southwest-style lasagna will satisfy any hungry crowd. It can be “stretched” with extra beans, and it’s super-easy to put together. You will love it!

Rice-Stuffed Acorn Squash

A Fall dish that uses freshly harvested squash with a hint of Oriental flavor that offers unexpected accents.

Jerk Chicken

A real feel-good carnival food. There’s nothing demanding about it, but the warmth of the spices punctuated by the fierce heat of the chillies will put a smile on your face.

Vegetable Skewers

Who said that berbecue is a meat-eater’s domain. The heat of the grill and a brush of olive oil brings out the smoky flavor of the vegetables.

Basil Pork

Basil Pork is a simple and delicious Asian dish. The basil gives the dish a nice flavor and also helps the dish attain its fresh taste and provides an appetizing aroma.

Chicken Curry

A common delicacy in South Asia, Southeast Asia, as well as in the Caribbean, chicken is sautéed with curry powder and simmered in an aromatic, slightly sweet curry sauce.

Eggplant Sandwich

Roasted eggplant in a French roll with feta cheese, basil and garlic mayonnaise. You can peel the eggplant, but the sandwiches are prettier with the deep-purple skin intact.

Turkey and Avocado BLT

Thinly sliced deli turkey, bacon, avocado and garden-ripe tomatoes make a perfect summertime sandwich.
