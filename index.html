WHY BE YOUR OWN CHEF?

You save more money by preparing your own meals at larger portions than dining at a bar or restaurant, not counting the fact that you might feel inclined to tip your server.

Meals prepared at home are almost always healthier. Restaurant items contain more fat, sugar, and carbohydrates. A large part of being your own chef is knowing what ingredients go into the food.

In the time it takes to drive to a restaurant, place your order, wait for your order, return home and serve the meal, you could have made a three-course meal from scratch with time to sit and enjoy. Also, cooking in bulk will allow you to have meals ready to go in just minutes.
